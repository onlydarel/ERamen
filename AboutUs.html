HISTORY

In 1910, a skilled ronin with a passion for culinary arts embarked on a journey across Japan,immersing himself in the diverse regional cuisines of the country.Inspired by his travels, he founded a ramen restaurant named ERamen, initially known as 'Hinode Ramen-ya,''which captured the essence of the rising sun in its flavors.With meticulous attention to detail and a masterful understanding of the art of ramen, the restaurant gainedimmense popularity over the years.In 2015, recognizing the growing appreciation for authentic Japanese cuisine, the owners of ERamen decided toexpand their culinary venture to Indonesia, bringing their exceptional ramen and culinary mastery to a newaudience, delighting Indonesian food enthusiasts with their exquisite flavors and the rich history behind eachbowl of ramen.

OUR OUTLET

Initially, ERamen was a modest ramen shop in a quaint neighborhood of Yokohama, Japan. However, its journey took a momentous turn when a renowned food critic stumbled upon the hidden gem and showered it with praise in a widely read article. Overnight, ERamen became the talk of the town, attracting long queues of eager customers. Capitalizing on the sudden popularity, the owners seized the opportunity to expand their reach, opening new outlets in major cities across Japan. With each new location, ERamen's reputation grew, as did its loyal customer base. The consistent quality of their flavorful broths, perfectly cooked noodles, and attention to detail set them apart, and soon, ERamen became a household name synonymous with exceptional ramen. Today, ERamen stands as a culinary empire, with numerous outlets not only in Japan but also in international locations, satisfying the cravings of ramen enthusiasts worldwide and upholding the legacy of its humble beginnings.
